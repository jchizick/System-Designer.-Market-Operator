Inspiration is unreliable.

Process is what remains when mood, motivation, and novelty disappear.

A good system does not require perfect conditions. It gives the operator something to return to when the signal gets messy.

This applies to trading.

It applies to design.

It applies to building anything that needs to survive longer than a burst of energy.

The work improves when the process becomes repeatable.

Repeatability is where momentum starts.
