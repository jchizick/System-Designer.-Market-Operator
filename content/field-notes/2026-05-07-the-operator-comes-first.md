A tool should be built around the operator's real state, not an idealized version of them.

Fatigue is part of the use case.

Emotion is part of the use case.

Doubt, pressure, impatience, and hesitation are part of the use case.

Most systems are designed as if the user is calm, focused, and rational at all times.

That is fiction.

A serious system accounts for the human being inside the loop.
