Signal degradation begins when a system tries to optimize for too many variables at once.

More data is not always more intelligence.

Every metric, module, feed, chart, and visual element must earn its place. If it does not improve judgment, it adds noise.

The danger is not a lack of information.

The danger is drowning the operator in inputs until the signal becomes indistinguishable from the static.

A clean system protects attention.
