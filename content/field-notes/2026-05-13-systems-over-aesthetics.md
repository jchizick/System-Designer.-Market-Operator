Aesthetic polish matters, but it cannot carry a weak system.

A good interface is not just something that looks sharp. It reveals structure. It reduces friction. It makes decisions easier.

The visual layer should serve the operating layer.

If the design is beautiful but the user still has to think too hard, the system is not finished.

The goal is not decoration.

The goal is operational clarity.
